Privacy Policy — Yeu Con

Last updated: April 2026

Yeu Con is a mobile application developed for parents. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data Collection

Yeu Con does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter (children's names, star transactions, rewards) is stored locally on your device using an on-device database (expo-sqlite). No data leaves your phone.

No Accounts

Yeu Con does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide an email address.

No Analytics or Tracking

The app does not use analytics services or any form of tracking.

No Internet Required

Yeu Con works entirely offline. It does not make network requests.

Children's Privacy

While Yeu Con is designed for parents to manage, children may view their star balance through the app. The app does not collect any data from children. We comply with COPPA and equivalent regulations by simply not collecting data at all.

Data Deletion

Uninstalling the app removes all stored data permanently.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at https://github.com/EugeneNguyen/yeu-con.
